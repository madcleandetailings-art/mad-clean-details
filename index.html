Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical detail take?

Most standard sedan details take 2–3 hours. Larger vehicles or add‑ons may add extra time.

Do you need access to electricity or water?

Currently we do not provide our own water or power. Please ensure there is access to a water spigot and a standard electrical outlet at your location.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, Cash App, Venmo and PayPal. Just let us know your preferred method when booking.
